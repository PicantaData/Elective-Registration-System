Elective Registration System DA-IICT

The Elective Registration System is designed to facilitate the selection and allotment of elective courses for students at DA-IICT. The system aims to streamline the process by allowing students to submit their course preferences and ensuring fair and efficient allotment based on predefined criteria.

How to Use This Website

  1. First, you need to login using your DA-IICT credentials.
  2. Once logged in, fill out the form displayed on the homepage with your details.
  3. Navigate to the 'Register' option.
  4. Under 'Register', go to each slot and provide your preferences. It is mandatory to give a preference for 'Preference 1', whether it is 'None' or the name of a course.
  5. After filling out your preferences for each slot, click on the 'Submit Preferences' button to save your choices.
